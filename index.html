Leaked Audio Recordings

Since the fall of her government, numerous phone conversations involving former Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina have been leaked on social media. These recordings offer a rare glimpse into her mindset during and after the July uprising. In one recording, Hasina claims that the army is being unfairly tried for merely following orders during the crackdown in July and August 2025.

“How do they show the victory sign after stealing?”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 29, 2025

The rhetoric in these calls often shifts towards vengeance. In another conversation, Hasina calls upon her supporters to "cleanse" the dirt of Bangladesh by beating or killing activists from opposing parties. She mentions creating a specific database to track incidents and exact revenge.

“I'm creating a list of everyone who was beaten... I will exact vengeance. I will cleanse them this time. My goal is to clean the dirt of Bangladesh.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 29, 2025

Her anger is frequently directed at Chief Adviser Muhammad Yunus. Accusing him of arresting her supporters, she threatens to pay him back in his own coin by putting him behind bars.

“He arrested my supporters. I will put him behind the bars too.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 28, 2025

In perhaps one of the most chilling recordings, she speaks with an Awami League leader about having a "license" to kill 227 people who filed cases against her. She rationalizes that the punishment for one murder is the same as for hundreds.

“The punishment for one case and the punishment for 225 cases are the same. All right, I will take that. But before that, I will count two hundred and a quarter. Keep that in mind.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 28, 2024

Defending her actions, she urges her supporters to fight back using the tactics of guerrilla warfare. She warns that anyone who touches her party "will burn their hands."

“If you touch fire, your hand will burn. We will make them understand that. Bangladesh was liberated by guerrilla warfare. So teach them a lesson.”
Sheikh Hasina • Jun 3, 2025

Hasina also attempts to incite the military. Referring to warrants issued against army officers, she questions their courage and reminds them that it was her government (and her father's) that purchased their tanks.

“How dare they issue a warrant against these officers? First Bangabandhu, and then I, bought them the tanks.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 18, 2025

She continues to rally her base by promising compensation. She instructs her followers to remember the faces of BNP-Jamaat members who attacked them, assuring them that properties of Yunus's "cronies" will be used for reparations.

“I'm warning BNP-Jamaat. Enough is enough. Remember all the faces. Spare none.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 10, 2025

The theme of guerrilla warfare recurs in her instructions. She tells victims of attacks to stop waiting for a return to power and instead take immediate action against their tormentors wherever they find them.

“Why do you forget that Bangladesh was liberated through guerrilla warfare? Adopt those tactics. Take action against them.”
Sheikh Hasina • Sept 23, 2025

In another call from late September, she orders her followers to "live like living beings" by identifying and resisting those who have harmed them.

“Take action against those who have harmed you. This is my order. To survive, you have to live like a living being.”
Sheikh Hasina • Sept 23, 2025

Following the ban on her party, Hasina expressed desperation. She stated that since the Awami League and Chhatra League are effectively banned, the "only option" left is to attack and kill members of the BNP and Jamaat.

“Since our party is banned, our only option is to attack and kill the BNP and Jamaat men. I don’t see any other option.”
Sheikh Hasina • Sept 28, 2025

Mobilization remains a key theme. In October, she urged all leaders and activists to "rise up" and protest with whatever resources they have available.

“All leaders and activists of the Awami League must rise up today. Protest with everything they have. Stand up.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 23, 2025

The former Prime Minister has also issued direct death threats against those she believes attempted to assassinate her, urging her followers to be strong rather than self-sacrificing.

“Those who tried take my life will have to die.”
Sheikh Hasina • Aug 10, 2025

Responding to the interim government's decision to ban the Awami League, Hasina labeled Muhammad Yunus a usurper. She vowed that he would one day be held accountable for his "unconstitutional" seizure of power.

“For this unlawful seizure of power, they will face consequences one day. The responsibility for the violence and murder will be held accountable.”
Sheikh Hasina • Nov 2, 2025

Her call for retribution is sometimes framed in religious terms. She invokes the principle of "an eye for an eye," inciting followers to burn down the houses of political rivals just as theirs were burned.

“The one who burns your house, why will he have his house intact? A hand for a hand, an eye for an eye.”
Sheikh Hasina • Nov 25, 2025

Even internal disruptions are met with harsh language. When some participants disrupted one of her calls, she labeled them "bastards of Pakistani ghosts" and threatened to deport them to Karachi.

“Some bad guys are entering the call... These are the bastards of Pakistani ghosts. I will put them on a ship and send them to Karachi.”
Sheikh Hasina • Aug 1, 2025

In a lengthy commentary on the armed forces, Hasina expressed frustration that the army has "lost its strength." She lamented the trials of officers involved in past crackdowns, linking their fate to the destruction of the army's global image.

“Has the army lost its strength? Can't they arrest them and take them away? Then they would’ve gotten an answer.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 23, 2025

Despite the overwhelming evidence of state violence during her tenure, Hasina continues to deny responsibility for the deaths during the July-August crackdown. She claims the orders to kill came from Dr. Yunus and Tarique Rahman as part of a "meticulous design."

“I did not order anyone to be killed; it was Dr Mohammad Yunus who gave the order. Tarek Zia is also involved in it.”
Sheikh Hasina • Nov 24, 2025

Finally, emphasizing unity among her scattered followers, she urged them to compile lists of those who harmed them. Reaffirming her stance, she demanded action "wherever you have it," invoking the Quran to justify retribution.

“An eye for an eye, a hand for a hand, keeping in mind the Quran. You will take action wherever you have it. This is what I want.”
Sheikh Hasina • Oct 29, 2025
