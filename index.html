The Leaked Tapes

Since the fall of her government, numerous phone conversations involving former Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina have been leaked on social media. These recordings offer a rare glimpse into her mindset during and after the July uprising.

“How do they show the victory sign after stealing? I will take care of each incident.”
Phone Call Oct 29, 2025

The rhetoric in these calls often shifts towards vengeance. In another conversation, Hasina calls upon her supporters to "cleanse" the dirt of Bangladesh.

“I'm creating a list of everyone who was beaten... I will exact vengeance. My goal is to clean the dirt of Bangladesh.”
Leaked Audio Oct 29, 2025

In perhaps one of the most chilling recordings, she speaks with an Awami League leader about having a "license" to kill 227 people who filed cases against her.

“The punishment for one case and the punishment for two hundred and a quarter are the same. All right, I will take that. But before that, I will count two hundred and a quarter. Keep that in mind.”
Telegram 2023
